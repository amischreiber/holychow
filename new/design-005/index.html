The Story

Holy Chow has been a cornerstone of the Silver Spring kosher community. What started as a simple dream to bring authentic Chinese flavors to kosher diners has grown into a beloved institution.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen is a testament to our commitment to quality. We source the freshest ingredients, prepare everything to order, and never cut corners. From our signature General Chow's Chicken to our hand-wrapped dumplings, every bite reflects our passion.

Whether you're ordering takeout for a quick family dinner, having food delivered for Shabbat prep, or catering a simcha, we're honored to be part of your table.
